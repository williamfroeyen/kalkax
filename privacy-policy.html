Privacy

1. Personal data

We like to keep things simple: Kalkax does not collect, store, or sell your personal data. We do not use cookies, tracking pixels, or any third-party analytics that identify you.

2. Technical data and hosting

While we don't "collect" data in the traditional sense, our server logs may process your IP address to deliver the website to you and maintain security. This is a technical necessity and is processed under the legal basis of Legitimate Interest (GDPR Art. 6(1)(f)). This data is not used to identify you personally.

3. Your rights

Because we do not store your data, there is usually nothing for us to provide, correct, or delete. However, under the GDPR, you have the right to inquire about our data practices at any time.

4. The future

If we ever decide to change our "no-data" stance or introduce third-party tools, we will update this policy before those changes take effect. We remain committed to full GDPR compliance.

5. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy occasionally for operational or legal reasons. The "Last Updated" date at the bottom will reflect the most recent version.
