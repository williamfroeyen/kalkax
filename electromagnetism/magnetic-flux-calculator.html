Flux:

Magnetic flux (Weber) can be calculated by multiplying the strength of the magnetic field (flux density, in Tesla), the area of the surface (m2), and the cosine of the angle between the magnetic field and the normal vector of the surface. The formula is: φ = B * A * cos(θ).

Magnetic flux is a measure of the magnetic field through a surface. The unit of magnetic flux is Weber (Wb).
