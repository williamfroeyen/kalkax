It is possible to use scientific notation with this calculator, such as 3.3e19 for 3.3 * 10^19. The calculator finds the radiant exitance (W/m²) or temperature (in kelvin) of a black body. The formula is: U = σT4. This law is known as Stefan-Boltzmann's law. Stefan-Boltzmann's constant (σ) is equal to 5.670 374 419 * 10^-8 W*m^(-2)*K^(-4).
